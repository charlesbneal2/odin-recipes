Enduring Steamed Meat

Description

Enduring Steamed Meat is a meal from the award-winning video game Breath of the Wild. It will fully refill Link's Stamina Wheel and grant Temporary Stamina.

Ingredients

  • One Raw Meat
  • One Endura Carrot

Steps

  1. Find an active cooking pot or fix one.
  2. Select the raw meat and endura carrot from Link's inventory to hold.
  3. Throw the ingredients into the cooking pot.
